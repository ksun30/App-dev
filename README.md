# Pepito Manaloto

## About the Series
**Pepito Manaloto** is a *Philippine situational comedy series* that tells the inspiring and humorous story of an ordinary man who suddenly becomes rich after winning the lottery.

---

### Main Characters
1. **Pepito Manaloto** – The kind-hearted lottery winner.
2. **Elsa Manaloto** – His supportive and loving wife.
3. **Patrick Manaloto** – Their smart and curious son.
4. **Kuwala & Chito** – Pepito’s funny and loyal friends.

---

### Why I Love the Series
- It teaches **family values** and **gratitude**.
- It has *realistic humor* that reflects Filipino life.
- It shows that **money isn’t everything** — happiness comes from kindness and love.

---

> “Ang tunay na yaman ay ang pamilyang nagmamahalan.”  
> — Pepito Manaloto

---

### Favorite Episode
My favorite episode is when Pepito helps his old neighbors even after becoming rich — a true reminder to *stay humble and generous*.
